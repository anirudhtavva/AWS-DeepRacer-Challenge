      The Toronto Turnpike is the sixth, and final, stop of the 2019 Virtual Circuit World Tour. This is the last chance in 2019 to win an expenses paid trip to Las Vegas, NV, to compete for the Championship Cup at re:Invent 2019!
      Start your engines

      1.

      Train a model

      Each virtual race will have its own competition track. Train you model on the training track most similar in look and shape to the competition track to get started.

      2.

      Race in the league

      Submit your model to participate in the virtual race. Your model will be ranked based on the average time it takes to complete a lap on the race track in our virtual simulator. Your time and rank will be displayed in the race leaderboard alongside other competitors.

      3.

      Experiment and win

      Tweak, retrain and resubmit your model to see if you can improve your ranking and take the top spot! Your lap time will earn you points, and could land you a prize!
